Chicken Burger

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 4 sesame seed buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill them for about 15 minutes, flipping once, until cooked through.
  3. Toast the buns lightly on both sides.
  4. Spread some mayonnaise on the bottom halves of the buns.
  5. Place a lettuce leaf, a tomato slice, a cheese slice, and a chicken breast on each bun.
  6. Cover with the top halves of the buns and enjoy!
